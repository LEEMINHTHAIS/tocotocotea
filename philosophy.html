A CUP OF TEA - A CUP OF HAPPINESS

Established in 2019. At TocoToco, we are committed to providing our customers with the most delicious and satisfying tea drinks. This is our mission and the core of the what we do! 2013 TocoToco started out small, but we've always been busy serving customers with the best quality ingredients, producing the tastiest drinks. In November 2013, the TocoToco Tea brand was officially established by Taco Vietnam Trading and Service Joint Stock Company. 2016 Tocotoco Tea breached the Saigon market, expanding the company scale and eventually becoming the leading milk tea company in Vietnam. Today, TocoToco has now branched out to about 150+ stores in Vietnam and is one of the most popular and trusted tea brands in the Vietnamese market. 2019 TocoToco Garden Grove is the second location in the entire United States and it's right here in Southern California! We are ready to make a mark in the American market, so please come and give us a try!
